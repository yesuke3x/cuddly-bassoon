Our Founder

Made with love.
Made by hand.

Maison Rosé was born from Eva Victoria's obsession with one perfect thing — strawberries at peak ripeness, transformed slowly over an open flame into something that tastes like a memory. No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just fruit, sugar, and time.

Every jar is tied by hand, sealed with intention, and delivered across Jakarta to homes that appreciate the difference between ordinary and extraordinary.
